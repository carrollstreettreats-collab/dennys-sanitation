What We Do

Our Services

From curbside garbage pickup to dumpster rentals and portable toilets, we've got your waste management needs covered.

Residential

Weekly curbside garbage pickup for homes throughout Lyon County. Dependable service rain or shine — just set your cart out by 7 AM on your pickup day.

Pickup Schedule:
Monday — Rock Rapids
Tuesday — George
Wednesday — Lester & Alvord
Friday — Doon & Larchwood

Commercial & Farm

Dumpster pickup service for farm and commercial customers throughout the area. We provide the container, schedule regular pickups, and handle all maintenance and servicing.

Recycling

Curbside recycling collection twice per month. Recycling helps keep our landfills from filling up too quickly and is a big part of doing our part for the environment.

Recycling Schedule:
1st & 3rd Thursdays — Rock Rapids & Larchwood
2nd & 4th Thursdays — George
Every other Monday — Lester, Doon & Alvord

Can Redemption

Bring in your Iowa-deposit cans and bottles to get your $0.05 per container refund. We accept cans, bottles, and 2-liter bottles from major brands.

Hours: Mon–Thu 12:00–5:00 PM (Closed Fri & Sat)
Requirements: Items must be clean, empty, and free from garbage. Bag cans in clear bags, box glass separately, and label with your name and phone number.

Portable Toilets

Portable toilet rental and servicing within a 40-mile radius of Rock Rapids. Perfect for events, celebrations, and work sites. Weekly maintenance available for extended projects.

Dumpsters & Roll-Offs

Dumpster and roll-off container rentals for construction, renovation, and cleanout projects. Available in multiple sizes to fit your needs.

Available Sizes:
1½ Yard — 5' W × 3' L × 4' H
4 Yard — 5.5' W × 7' L × 4' H
6 Yard — 5.5' W × 10' L × 4' H
20 Yard — 8' W × 19' L × 4' H
30 Yard — 8' W × 22' L × 5' H

Billing

Payment Policies

Please review our payment policies below. Contact us with any questions.

New Long-Term Service

Denny's Sanitation requires one of two things to set up a new long-term service: a $50 deposit that stays in the account for one year (after a year of good payments, the $50 is credited back to the customer's account), or enrollment in auto-pay via credit card or ACH. If auto-pay is set up, no deposit is required.

New Short-Term Service

A down payment via credit card is required. This covers the delivery fee and first service on the container. The credit card stays on file until services are completed and the account is paid in full. We will not charge for additional services after the down payment is used up unless the account is 2 months past due, at which point Denny's Sanitation reserves the right to charge the card.

Roll-Offs

A down payment via credit card is required. The amount is determined based on location and current pricing. The credit card stays on file until services are completed. After the down payment is used up, Denny's Sanitation reserves the right to auto-run the credit card for services owed, either monthly or when services are completed.

Accounts with Prior Late Fees

Denny's Sanitation reserves the right to not extend credit on accounts that have previously had a late fee or past-due balance. A down payment via credit card may be required, and the card will stay on file until services are completed. Denny's Sanitation reserves the right to auto-run the card before any service is performed.
