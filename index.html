Redemption

Can Redemption

Bring in your Iowa-deposit cans and bottles for your $0.05 per container refund.

Hours: Mon–Thu 12–5 PM (Closed Fri & Sat)

Accepted brands: 7-Up, Coke, Pepsi, Sam's, Shasta, Clover Valley, Old Milwaukee, Miller, Budweiser, Casey's, Best Choice, Best Yet, Shurfine, Our Family, Fastco, American Fare.

Not accepted: Arnold Palmer Half/Half Tea, Arizona Tea, Bae, Black Rifle Coffee, Body Armor, Brisk, De Mi Pais (DMP), Dole, Fareway, Folgers 1850, fruit & vegetable tin cans/bottles, Gatorade, Jumex, Krogers, Lipton Iced Tea, milk jugs, Nestea, nutritional supplements, Powerade, Prime Plastic, Propel, Pure Leaf Tea, water bottles, V8.

Rules: Items must be CLEAN, EMPTY, and FREE FROM GARBAGE. Items cannot be crushed or destroyed. Cans must be in clear bags. Glass must be boxed separately. Label all bags with your name and phone number.

Important: If you have an overdue garbage account, your redemption money will be credited to your account and not paid out in cash.

New Long-Term Service

We require either a $50 deposit that stays in the account for one year (credited back after a year of good payments), or enrollment in auto-pay via credit card or ACH. If auto-pay is set up, no deposit is required.

New Short-Term Service

A down payment via credit card covers the delivery fee and first service. The card stays on file until paid in full. We will not charge further unless the account is 2 months past due.

Roll-Offs

A credit card down payment is required, amount based on location and pricing. After the down payment is used up, we reserve the right to auto-run the card monthly or upon completion.

Previously Late Accounts

We reserve the right to not extend credit on previously late accounts. A credit card down payment may be required, and we may auto-run the card before any service is performed.
