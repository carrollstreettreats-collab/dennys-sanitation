What We Do

Our Services

From curbside garbage pickup to dumpster rentals and portable toilets — we handle it all.

01

Residential

Weekly curbside garbage pickup for homes throughout Lyon County. Dependable service rain or shine — just set your cart out by 7 AM on your pickup day.

Pickup Schedule:
Mon — Rock Rapids / Tue — George
Wed — Lester & Alvord
Fri — Doon & Larchwood
02

Commercial & Farm

Dumpster pickup service for farm and commercial customers throughout the area. We provide the container, schedule regular pickups, and handle maintenance.

03

Recycling

Curbside recycling collection twice per month. Doing our part to keep landfills from filling up too quickly.

Schedule:
1st & 3rd Thu — Rock Rapids & Larchwood
2nd & 4th Thu — George
Every other Mon — Lester, Doon & Alvord
04

Can Redemption

Bring in your Iowa-deposit cans and bottles to get your $0.05 per container refund. We accept major brands.

Hours: Mon–Thu 12–5 PM (Closed Fri & Sat)
Items must be clean, empty, and free from garbage. Bag cans in clear bags, box glass separately.
05

Portable Toilets

Portable toilet rental and servicing within a 40-mile radius of Rock Rapids. Perfect for events, celebrations, and work sites with optional weekly maintenance.

06

Dumpsters & Roll-Offs

Dumpster and roll-off rentals for construction, renovation, and cleanout projects.

Sizes: 1½ yd · 4 yd · 6 yd · 20 yd · 30 yd

Payment Policies

Please review our payment policies below.

New Long-Term Service

We require either a $50 deposit that stays in the account for one year (credited back after a year of good payments), or enrollment in auto-pay via credit card or ACH. If auto-pay is set up, no deposit is required.

New Short-Term Service

A down payment via credit card covers the delivery fee and first service. The card stays on file until paid in full. We will not charge further unless the account is 2 months past due.

Roll-Offs

A credit card down payment is required, amount based on location and pricing. After the down payment is used up, we reserve the right to auto-run the card monthly or upon completion.

Prior Late Fees

We reserve the right to not extend credit on previously late accounts. A credit card down payment may be required, and we may auto-run the card before any service is performed.
